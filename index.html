Instructions

Getting Started

Welcome to Fairway Command — The Ultimate League System. Use the sidebar to navigate between sections. Select your role (Player, Scorer, Admin) in the header to see relevant sections.

Roles

  1. Player: View dashboard, leaderboard, schedule, analytics, courses, chat, and instructions
  2. Scorer: All Player access plus Enter Scores and Scramble Events
  3. Admin: Full access including Payments, Rainout Manager, Handicap Formula, Score Card Printing, and League Settings

How to Enter Scores

  1. Switch to Scorer or Admin role
  2. Go to Enter Scores from the sidebar
  3. Select the player, course, side, and date
  4. Enter hole-by-hole scores or just the total
  5. Click Submit Score

9-Hole Scorecards

  1. Go to Admin section and find Print Score Cards
  2. Select the match week to see all pairings
  3. Preview scorecards with handicap stroke dots
  4. Print on 7x5 card stock for on-course use

Scramble Events

  1. Go to Scramble Events to view upcoming 18-hole scrambles
  2. Admins can create new events and assign teams
  3. Teams are balanced by combined handicap

Custom Handicap Formula

Current Formula

HCP = (Avg of Best 8 of Last 20 Scores - Course Rating) × 0.96 × (113 / Slope Rating)

This formula calculates the 9-hole league handicap index. Adjust the parameters below to customize for your league.
