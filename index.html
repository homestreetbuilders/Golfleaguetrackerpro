      Getting Started

      Welcome to GolfLeague TrackerPro! Here's how to use the app to manage your golf league.

      How to Enter Scores

      1. Go to the Scores section from the sidebar
      2. Select the player from the dropdown
      3. Choose the course and date of the round
      4. Enter the total score
      5. Click Submit Score to record the round

      League Rules & Scoring

      1. Weekly rounds are played every Saturday morning
      2. USGA handicap system is used for all players
      3. Teams earn points based on head-to-head matchups each week
      4. The top 4 teams qualify for the end-of-season playoff
      5. Substitutes are allowed with 48 hours advance notice to the admin

      Payments

      1. Season fee is $150 per player
      2. Fees must be paid before the 3rd round of the season
      3. Payment status can be checked in the Payments section
      4. Contact the league admin for payment arrangements

      Using the Chat

      1. Navigate to League Chat under the Social section
      2. View messages from other league members
      3. Type your message and click Send to post
      4. Use the chat for scheduling, announcements, and general discussion

      Admin & Scorecards

      1. Navigate to the Admin section from the sidebar
      2. Select the match week from the dropdown
      3. Click Preview Scorecards to view all scorecards on screen
      4. Click Print All Scorecards to open a print-ready window
      5. Stroke dots show which player receives strokes on which holes
